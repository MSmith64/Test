CONTROLLED REMOTE VIEWING
STAGE 1

Ideograms & Gestalt

In Stage 1 the viewer receives the very first impulse from the signal line — the overall “gestalt” or nature of the target.

Objective

Produce a single, rapid ideogram that captures the site’s fundamental shape or feeling. No analysis is permitted.

Procedure

  1. Write the coordinate.
  2. Place pen on paper and allow the hand to move instantly and automatically.
  3. The resulting line drawing is the ideogram.
  4. Immediately label it with a single letter descriptor (A = feeling, B = gestalt).
  5. Declare any AOL and move on.
Example Ideogram descriptors:
Rising angle across, down, solid → B: Mountain / Structure
Curved horizontal loop → B: Body of water

Stage 1 must be executed in under three seconds. Speed prevents the conscious mind from interfering.
