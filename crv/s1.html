STAGE 1

S1 – Ideogram & Basic Gestalt

What happens in Stage 1?

The very first unconscious response to the target coordinate. Your hand draws an ideogram — a quick, spontaneous squiggle — before your conscious mind can interfere.

Procedure:
1. Write the target ID at the top of your paper.
2. Hold pen lightly.
3. Say “S1” aloud.
4. Let your hand move instantly — 1–2 seconds only.
5. Label the ideogram with one or two words (e.g., “rising,” “angular,” “watery”).
